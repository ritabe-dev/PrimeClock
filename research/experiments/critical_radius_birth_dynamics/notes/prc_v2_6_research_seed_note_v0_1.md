# PRC v2.6 Research Seed Note v0.1

## Goal

Purpose: start v2.6 as a source-only Gate R research line without changing the
published v2.5 theorem release. Current completion is about 0% for v2.6 and the
first Gate R starter target is about 1 slice away.

The initial question is not whether B8 is already a public theorem. The initial
question is which next route is technically worth pursuing:

- B8 finite extension;
- prefix-only predictor;
- obstruction theorem;
- negative diagnostic.

## Non-claims

v2.6 Gate R makes no public claim, no DOI claim, no B8 theorem, no B8 full graph,
no general predictor, and no asymptotic law. It does not modify the v2.5 public
theorem release, root DOI metadata, release registry, or release-specific
citation metadata.

## Candidate Routes

`B8 finite extension` asks whether a full or near-full B8 materialized universe
is computationally practical and audit-ready. It remains deferred until the
family count, lift-row estimate, runtime estimate, and disk-size estimate are
known.

`prefix-only predictor` asks whether any terminal-margin-free signal can
separate likely close rows from controls. It remains deferred until a signal is
observed without using the terminal signed containment margin.

`obstruction theorem` asks whether the exact-hull and single-gap precursor
structure can be promoted from finite support diagnostics toward a clearer
lemma. It can continue if the structure remains strong under v2.6 checks.

`negative diagnostic` is the correct outcome if the B8 universe is too costly,
prefix-only features remain weak, and obstruction structure does not sharpen.

## B8 Feasibility Questions

- What is the expected B8 family count before full graph generation?
- What is the expected lift-row count before full graph generation?
- What runtime and memory budget would a full B8 materialization require?
- What disk budget would the committed certificate tables require?
- Which existing source-only B8 stress-control artifacts are available?
- Which B8 terms are safely non-claims rather than public theorem evidence?

## Stop Conditions

Stop the B8 finite-extension route if the feasibility audit cannot populate the
family-count, lift-row, runtime, and disk-size estimate fields without generating
a full B8 graph.

Stop the prefix-only predictor route if the only complete separator remains the
terminal signed containment certificate.

Stop the obstruction theorem route if exact-hull or single-gap precursor
structure does not produce a clearer finite lemma candidate.
