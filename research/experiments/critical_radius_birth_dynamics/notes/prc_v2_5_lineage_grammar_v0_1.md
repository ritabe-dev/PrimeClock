# PRC v2.5 Lineage Grammar Seed

The lineage grammar compresses exact residual state histories into transition
sequences such as:

```text
start>split>trim>close
start>split>trim>phase-failing non-close
```

The purpose is not to invent a large taxonomy.  The purpose is to test whether
close-prone and persistent non-close lineages have measurably different
transition sequences before the final signed phase-margin decision.

This note is diagnostic-only until the v2.5 Gate R decision table is updated.
