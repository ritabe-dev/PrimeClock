# PRC v2.3 Research Review Note v0.1

Status: retrospective research-review gate for the v2.3 internal candidate.

## Core idea

The v2.3 research story is the critical radius spectrum together with the
gap-aperture birth formula. The interesting point is that a birth is not
explained by old residual gap size alone: it also depends on whether the next
prime q-grid phase lands inside the old gap's dual containment window.

## Claim shape

The critical-radius level-set identity is a definition-level reorganization of
the finite covering problem. The spectra for k=4,5 and the B_5/B_6/B_7 birth
classifications are finite checked results. Near-miss and alignment data are
diagnostics, not predictors by themselves. Broader transition graphs, null
models, and larger layers remain future work.

## Why now

The package has a coherent mechanism-level story: B_5/B_6/B_7 are unique strict
single-gap births, and each checked birth parent has exactly one admissible
next-prime remainder. That is enough structure to package the current finite
candidate before broadening the research surface.

## Stop line

This candidate stops before B_8, structured null models, residual gap
transition graphs, and full active-prime taxonomy. Those directions may be
valuable, but including them here would mix the v2.3 mechanism candidate with
v2.4-style genealogy work.

## Risks

The current results are finite and should not be read as asymptotic or
prime-distribution claims. Related-work boundaries still need care before a
public release. The theorem note is suitable for internal review but still
needs more public-facing formalization before citation.

## Decision

package as candidate
