## Boundaries Inherited From v2.7.1

Carry these boundaries forward unless a later proof explicitly changes them:

- PRC claims are internal to the stated circular-arc model.
- A direct one-prime q-lift is not the same as a full sequential insertion of
  intermediate primes.
- Recorded birth rows audits are implementation/data consistency evidence, not
  the proof of a general theorem.
- Do not claim a prime-gap theorem outside the PRC model.
- Do not claim an asymptotic law or general predictor without a separate proof.
- Do not present finite audit output as a mechanically verified proof.
