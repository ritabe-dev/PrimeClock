# PRC v2.5 Research Seed Note v0.1

Status: source-only seed for the next candidate line. This is not a candidate
package, public release, DOI artifact, or public theorem claim.

## Purpose

Use the v2.4 source-only bridge as a starting point for a broader research line
without treating v2.4 as a public result. The v2.5 question is no longer only
whether a final sibling lift closes. It asks why positive phase margin appears,
why capacity-satisfied families fail, and whether close-prone residual
lineages can be predicted before the final prime lift.

## Inherited Diagnostics

The following v2.4 diagnostics may be reused as design input:

- signed phase-margin separation as a finite diagnostic tool;
- capacity false positives among sibling-lift families;
- non-birth sibling controls and transition itineraries;
- residual lineage and transition graph tables;
- arithmetic refinements such as width-normalized `k=2, r=3` survival and
  reflection-paired remainder concentration.

These are not inherited as public claims. If they become part of a v2.5
candidate package, they must be regenerated, checked, and documented under v2.5
file names.
