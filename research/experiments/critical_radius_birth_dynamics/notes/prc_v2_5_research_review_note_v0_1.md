# PRC v2.5 Research Review Note

## Core idea

The v2.5 research line treats PRC as residual gap dynamics with a checked
finite aperture-orbit separator.  The object of study is the exact residual
set `R_k(r)`, its layer-to-layer transition history, and the final signed
aperture margin induced by the next prime grid.

The candidate direction is:

```text
Checked transition scopes show that capacity admits many false positives, while
positive signed aperture-orbit margin separates close lifts from capacity
non-close controls.
```

## Claim shape

- Candidate claim: in checked PRC transition scopes, capacity admits many false
  positives, while positive signed aperture-orbit margin separates close lifts
  from capacity non-close controls.
- Checked support lemma: in checked scopes, multi-component parent residual sets
  are exact-hull obstructed, and every checked close row has a single-gap
  precursor.
- Stress control: B8 remains a source-only stress control for the separator,
  not a public theorem.
- Non-claim: no B8 full graph, no B8 public theorem, no asymptotic law, no
  general prediction theorem, and no public release in this slice.

## Why now

v2.3 fixed a public finite birth mechanism, and v2.4 built source-only
transition and phase diagnostics.  v2.5 now has enough exact checks to move
from open exploration to an internal candidate line: historical B5/B6/B7
families calibrate the separator, B8 selected controls stress it, and exact
hull obstruction keeps multi-component close explanations out of scope.

## Stop line

This Gate R slice stops at an internal candidate line.  It does not create a
GitHub Release, Zenodo archive, B8 full run, full null model, public theorem, or
public DOI claim.  Gate P must decide any public-facing release separately.

## Risks

- The separator may still be read as a finite decision rule unless the
  residual-dynamics framing is kept explicit.
- B8 evidence is selected stress-control evidence, not exhaustive coverage.
- Exact hull obstruction is a support lemma for checked scopes, not an
  asymptotic structure theorem.
- External review may ask for smaller derived tables if full source-only
  controls are too large to inspect manually.

## Decision

package as candidate

Gate R is closed for v2.5.  The result is ready for an internal candidate
package line because the aperture-orbit separator, B8 stress controls, and exact
hull support lemma are now checked finite diagnostics with clear non-claim
boundaries.  Public claim, public release, and DOI work remain outside this
slice.
